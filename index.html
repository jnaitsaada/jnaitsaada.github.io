I am…

…an Applied Scientist at Amazon , part of the APP (Analytics, Performance & Programs) Machine Learning and Engineering team, focusing on optimisation and machine learning problems.

Previously, I was an Advanced AI Scientist at BenevolentAI working on machine learning solutions for drug discovery.

I was a DPhil candidate part of the Statistical Genetics group working with Prof. Pier Palamara at the Statistics Departement at the University of Oxford and I recently graduated with a PhD in Statistics.

Prior to that, I studied at Telecom Paris and Eurecom , and I was then a graduate student enrolled in the MSc By Research in Statistics at the University of Oxford. You can find my Masters thesis here.
