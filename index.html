I am…

…an Applied Mathematics PhD Candidate in the Data Science group of the Mathematical Institute at the University of Oxford advised by Prof. Jared Tanner.

My PhD research focuses on theories of Deep Learing. In particular, I am interested in the study of infinitely wide neural networks and possible optimal initialisation schemes.

I also have some interests in Geometrical Machine Learning methods, as such as applications of Deep Learning models to solve public health issues.
